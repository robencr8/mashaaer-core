Theme 2: Falling Stars

About This Theme

The Falling Stars theme creates a serene and magical night sky experience. It features dynamically generated stars that move across the screen, creating a peaceful ambiance while maintaining the interactive elements users love about Mashaaer Feelings.

This alternative theme offers a different aesthetic while preserving all the functionality of the application. It's perfect for users who prefer a calmer, more meditative experience.
