Getting Started

What is Robin AI?
Robin AI is an advanced AI assistant from a parallel cosmic realm. It combines voice recognition, emotion detection, face recognition, and contextual understanding to provide a personalized and adaptive experience.
How do I talk to Robin?
Tap the "Sonic Portal" button on the home screen to activate voice communication. Speak clearly, and Robin will process your voice and respond accordingly.

Key Features

What is Emotional Cosmos?
Emotional Cosmos tracks and visualizes your emotional patterns over time, helping Robin adapt its responses to your emotional state.
What are Face Profiles?
Robin can recognize faces and associate them with profiles. This allows for personalized greetings and responses when it sees a familiar face.
For the best experience, use Robin in a well-lit environment with minimal background noise. This helps with both face recognition and voice clarity.

Troubleshooting

Voice recognition isn't working
Make sure you've granted microphone permissions in your browser. Try speaking clearly and in a quiet environment. If issues persist, try refreshing the page.
Camera access issues
Ensure you've granted camera permissions in your browser. If the camera isn't activating, try closing other applications that might be using the camera and refresh the page.
